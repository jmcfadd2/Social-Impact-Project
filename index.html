Gentrification and Minority Communities

Why does it matter?

Gentrification can displace people from minority communities and this can lead to negative outcomes for their livlihoods and safety

Where does it happen?

Gentrification happens in major cities across the nation. Philadelphia has experienced gentrification in the Graduate Hospital and Northern Liberties communities

What can be done?

More property tax controls can be put in place to keep those who already have homes in a community protected from the rising costs associated with nearby rising property values
